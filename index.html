 Basic Hardware

Buy any ordinary laptop with atleast 8 GB of RAM, and if possible add another 8 GB RAM stick (check compatibility).

When my parents learned that I had submitted an application for Computer Science, they wanted me to get a laptop. I was persuaded to purchase a MacBook Air (in 2018), but my aspirations of having any Apple product were dashed due to a silly "purchase restriction."

I had no choice but to use a Windows laptop. I eventually purchased a Dell laptop with high-end hardware (and a finger print sensor üòâ, which was new at the time), and I have no complaints about it to this day.. The (upgraded) specs:

  • (8 x 2) GB DDR4-2666 MHz RAM
  • 2.5 GHz Intel Core i5-7200 7th Gen processor
  • 1TB 5400rpm Serial ATA hard drive
  • Added: 1 TB Samsung 860 EVO SSD (Borrowed from laptop)
  • Graphics Card: AMD Radeon R5 M315 2GB Graphics (My friend gave me his graphics card as he was moving out, so I do not know what is installed at this moment).
